Autumn festival

Falling into that mood

FALL CELEBRATION - THROUGH COFFEE, MUSIC AND COMMUNITY


The special atmosphere of autumn enchants us all - so much so that we have prepared a two-day series of special programs to celebrate it. Because at some point, we really need togetherness, celebration, warmth, and love.

That's what it's about.

What you can expect?

BODY WARMING DELICITIES

Pumpkin spice and chai latte in all quantities, themed savory and sweet delicacies, coffee cocktail bar on Saturdays, pretzels, pumpkin baked with honey and much more.

HEARTWARMING MEETINGS


Connections, pleasant conversations, live music.

MAKE YOUR FAVORIT MUG 

Craft workshops, pottery ceramic painting.

MOVIE NIGHT 

Watching a movie together under the stars and a warm blanket.

GARDROBE FAIR 

Warm sweaters, autumn scarves, jackets are waiting for a new owner.

MACRAME HOME DECOR 

Make a macrame decoration for your home.

We are preparing a lot of special things both days. Take a look and decide which day you want to be with us... or join us on both!
